About Me



Hi! My name is Jenny Berry. I had dreamed since childhood of being a pastry chef, but life took me down many other paths before I finally made the jump into setting up my own business. Luca Loaves is a new patisserie I opened in Sydney CBD.

About Luca Loaves



At Luca Loaves, we take pride in moulding the perfect creation born from simple ingredients. We share a passion and love for our craft like no other. We respect the history of artisan bakers that have come before us, and we honour their heritage with a fresh and creative approach. The communities we operate in demand that “homemade” means “homemade”. This is an authentic promise because this is where the real inspiration comes from. We bring raw ingredients to life, by combining them with passion and love to create authentic, honest baked goods that inspire a desire to bake more. We’re 100% committed to bringing you freshly baked bread made from scratch every single day using only the best ingredients, like 100% Aussie wheat fresh from the farm.
